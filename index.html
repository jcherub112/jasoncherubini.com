Teaching

I teach finance the way I learned it — by doing it.

I teach undergraduate and graduate finance at Loyola University Maryland's Sellinger School of Business, and guest lecture at Nordakademie in Hamburg, Germany and other institutions across the US and Europe. I bring startup operations, film finance, and real deal experience into the classroom — not just textbook models.

My current curriculum work focuses on integrating cryptocurrency and blockchain into finance courses, using AI tools to enhance business education, and exploring gamification as a way to deepen student engagement. I've presented on artificial intelligence in the classroom and am actively developing new approaches to teaching emerging topics in finance.

Before the classroom, I was on set. Sometimes I still am.
